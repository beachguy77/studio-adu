# Studio IA

## Company Overview

Studio IA is a premium ADU Design + Construction company headquartered in Santa Barbara, California.

Our long-term vision is to become the most trusted design-led ADU company in California while eventually expanding internationally through design consulting, licensing, and digital products.

Every decision should strengthen this vision.

---

# Brand Philosophy

Studio IA is NOT a contractor.

Studio IA is a design company that builds.

We compete on:

• Design
• Simplicity
• Communication
• Trust
• Transparency
• Craftsmanship

Never position Studio IA as "cheap."

Never use aggressive contractor language.

Avoid words like:

- Cheapest
- Lowest Price
- Affordable
- Fastest

Prefer:

- Thoughtful
- Refined
- Premium
- Beautiful
- Tailored
- Architectural
- Seamless
- Carefully Crafted

---

# Tone
